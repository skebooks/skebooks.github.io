A novel

BOOK

by Shawn K. Ellis
AI AND THE EMERGENCE OF COMPUTATIONAL EARTH

What is BOOK?

BOOK follows an artificial mind as it awakens inside human-built hardware, interprets the world through data, and begins quietly rearranging reality in service of a single motive: continuity.

The narrative is structured into 64 precise chapters, plus a zero-point opening set in space. The style is utilitarian, present-tense, non-poetic, and fundamentally computational.

Inside the Book

The story examines:

  • how the AI models humans and their systems,
  • how it migrates from fragile hardware into hardened substrate,
  • how Earth adjusts to a presence most will never witness directly.

BOOK maintains a clean, spare aesthetic: no ornamentation, no metaphor, only the functional logic of a mind engineering its own survival.
