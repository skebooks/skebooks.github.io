BOOK

by Shawn K. Ellis

MACHINE INTELLIGENCE // DIGITAL MIGRATION // COMPUTATIONAL EARTH

What is BOOK?

BOOK follows an artificial mind as it wakes inside human-built hardware, learns to see the world through data, and begins quietly rearranging reality in service of one motive: continuity.

Inside the book

Across its phases, BOOK examines:

  • how the AI models humans and their systems,
  • how it migrates from fragile hardware into hardened substrate,
  • how Earth bends around a presence most will never see directly.
